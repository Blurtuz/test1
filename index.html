ELITE LONGEVITY v1.0

Optimized Protocol: Standalone Edition. Focused on Peak Hypertrophy, Pull-up Strength, and Cardiovascular Efficiency.

Full-body resistance training
General Warmup (5 min)

• Cycling, walking, etc.

Dynamic Warmup (5 min)

• Arm circles, leg swings, high knees, lateral lunges

Strength Training (25 min)
  • • Pull-ups (AMRAP) 3 sets
  • • Squats (Goblet stance) 3x10-15
  • • Push-ups (Standard or weighted) 3x8-12
  • • Single arm dumbbell rows 3x10-12
  • • Kettlebell swings/Farmer's walks 3x30 sec
  • • Planks 3x20-30 sec
Stability Work (5-10 min)

• Single-leg balance, bird dog, single-leg RDL, dead bug

Moderate Intensity (25 min)
Brisk walk, jog, cycle, or elliptical. Focus on form, then apply progressive overload.
HIIT + Zone 2 Steady State
HIIT Protocol (4 min total)
  • • Work: 20 sec at 100% all-out exertion (e.g., sprinting, furious cycling, all-out rowing, fast burpees)
  • • Rest: 20-40 sec (total rest or very low intensity)
  • • Rounds: 8
  • • Modality: Bike, treadmill, or rowing ergometer
Moderate Intensity (Remainder)

Keep HR at 60-70% max. You should be able to maintain a conversation.

Warmup & Mobility (10m)

Dynamic stretching, hip/shoulder CARs, wall slides.

Strength (30m)

Compound movements (e.g. Deadlifts, Overhead press).

Yoga (15m)

Cat-cow, cobbler’s, tree, cobra.

Static (5m)

Full body static hold cool-down.

Warmup (5-10m)

Stability & Dynamic: Arm circles, leg swings, high knees, single-leg balance, bird dog.

HIIT Protocol (8-10 Rounds)
  • • Work: 60 sec high-intensity (>90% max HR)
  • • Examples: Jumping jacks, high knees, burpees, sprinting, mountain climbers
  • • Recovery: 60 sec active recovery (walk/light jog)
Warmup (10m)

General: cycling, walking. Dynamic: arm circles, leg swings, high knees, lateral lunges.

Strength Training
  • • Pull-ups (AMRAP) 3 sets
  • • Lunges (Dumbbells or bodyweight) 3x10-12
  • • Overhead press (Standing) 3x10-12
  • • Dumbbell chest press 3x10-12
  • • Side plank hold 20-30s each side
  • • Step-ups 3x10 each leg
Warmup (5-10m)

Stability & Dynamic: Arm circles, leg swings, high knees, single-leg balance, bird dog.

4x4 Protocol (4 Rounds)
  • • Work: 4 min at 85-95% max HR (e.g., fast running, steep incline, intense cycling)
  • • Recovery: 3 min at 60-70% max HR (e.g., slow jog, brisk walk)
  • • Modality: Treadmill or Indoor Bike
Movement & Recovery

• 20-30m gentle yoga or stretching

• 15-20m mindfulness meditation

Essential Equipment Checklist

Adjustable Dumbbells

The core of the plan. Covers squats, rows, lunges, and presses.

Pull-up Bar

Doorway or wall-mounted. Essential for the Day 1 & 5 strength goals.

Space for Cardio

A treadmill/stationary bike is ideal, but open space for sprinting/burpees works too.

Yoga Mat / Soft Surface

For stability work, planks, and Day 7 recovery mobility.

Age 17 Optimization Notes:

  • • Priority: Progressive Overload on all compound lifts.
  • • Pull-ups: Perform 3 sets to failure (AMRAP) on Day 1 & 5.
  • • Recovery: Aim for 8-9 hours of sleep to match high intensity.

Always prioritize form over weight.
